Computer Science SL Notes

Here Computer Science students can find there holy grail -- the complete revision guide for CS SL. Everything you need to know for your theory paper 1 is included on this. Definitions, a resource hub, and practice questions with solutions are all included!

Business SL Notes

Here is an excellent questionbank that contains a lot of subjects that you can use on the go to help revise. It has filters for topic-to-topic and paper-to-paper, making your revision a whole lot easier.

Here is a very handy pseduocode simulator -- a syntax that you should get comfortable with if you are doing computer science in the IB or in later life! This website has numerous problem sets to explantations all in order for you to prepare for your exams.
